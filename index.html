Our Story

A bakery built on
freshness, family & flavor.

For over 15 years, Atul Bakery has been baking happiness into every bite. From our famous eggless pastries to our signature house specials, every product is crafted fresh, daily — the way it should be.

We're proud to serve our community with 100% eggless treats, dedicated Jain and Swaminarayan options, and an ever-growing menu of cakes, snacks, puffs, and shakes that bring the taste of home to Floral Park.
